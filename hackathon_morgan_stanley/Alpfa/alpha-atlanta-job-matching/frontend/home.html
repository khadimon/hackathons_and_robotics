About ALPFA Atlanta

ALPFA (Association of Latino Professionals For America) is the largest Latino association for business professionals and students, with chapters nationwide and over 95,000 members. ALPFA Atlanta provides professional development, networking opportunities, and career resources to empower and develop Latino leaders.
